Week 1

Awareness & Education

Kick-starting the mindset change.

  • Awareness session including 1-minute recycling video
  • Share simple plastic reduction facts
  • Introduce campaign and waste segregation
  • Banners: "Reduce Single-Use Plastics", "Use Correct Recycling Bin", "Bring Reusable Items"
  • Pledge Board participation
Week 2

Reverse Vending Challenge

Taking action through collection.

  • Activity: Place clear plastic collection drums/boxes across sites.
  • Goal: Collect plastic waste from site and household to maximize collection by end of July.
  • Tracking: Track total weight of plastic waste collected per site.
Week 3

Site-to-Site Sustainability

Implementing practical everyday practices.

Practical Ideas: Use wood instead of plastic sheets, setup buffet for labor workers instead of packaged meals, use wooden cutlery.

Daily Update: Each site submits 1 simple update/photo weekly.

Week 4

Plastic to Purpose

"Turning Waste into Value"

Teams collect plastic waste generated in offices and sites and transform it into something useful for the workplace.

  • Tool holders
  • Planter boxes
  • Break area decorations
  • Awareness displays & Safety sign frames
